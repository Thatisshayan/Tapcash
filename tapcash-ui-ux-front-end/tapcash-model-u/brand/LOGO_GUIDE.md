# TapCash Logo Guide

## Final direction: Wallet Tap

The final logo direction uses a rounded wallet, a tap ripple, and a cash edge.

Why it works:

- Wallet represents payout/cashout.
- Tap ripple represents simple action and the name TapCash.
- Cash edge represents rewards.
- Rounded geometry keeps it friendly and app-icon ready.

## Usage

Use the horizontal logo in the website header. Use the icon for favicon, app icon, mobile splash, and small nav contexts.

## Avoid

- Full white hand cursor in the final logo.
- Casino imagery.
- Overly sharp esports lettering.
- Tiny dollar symbols that disappear at small sizes.
- Heavy glow effects baked into the SVG.
