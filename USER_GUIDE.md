## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit TapCash.com**
   - Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner

2. **Enter Your Information**
   - Email address
   - Strong password (min 8 characters, include numbers and symbols)
   - Display name

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - You're ready to start earning!

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click on your profile picture
3. Add optional information:
   - Phone number (for Interac payouts)
   - Payment preferences
   - Notification settings

## Earning Rewards

### Finding Offers

1. **Browse Available Offers**
   - Visit the "Earn" page
   - Filter by category: Games, Surveys, Shopping, Apps, Videos
   - Sort by reward amount or difficulty

2. **Offer Details**
   - **Reward**: Amount you'll earn
   - **Difficulty**: Easy, Medium, or Hard
   - **Time**: Estimated completion time
   - **Requirements**: What you need to do

- Click "Start Offer" button
- Read the instructions carefully

- Start your next offer!

## TapScore System

### What is TapScore?

TapScore is your trust rating (0-100). Higher scores unlock:
- Better offers
- Higher rewards
- Faster payouts
- Exclusive bonuses

### How to Increase Your TapScore

✅ **Complete offers honestly** (+5 points)
✅ **Verify your email** (+10 points)
✅ **Add phone number** (+5 points)
✅ **Complete profile** (+5 points)
✅ **Maintain good standing** (+1 point/week)
✅ **Refer friends** (+10 points per referral)

### TapScore Levels

- **0-25**: Beginner - Basic offers
- **26-50**: Bronze - More offers available
- **51-75**: Silver - Premium offers unlocked
- **76-90**: Gold - Exclusive high-value offers
- **91-100**: Platinum - VIP status, best offers

## Troubleshooting

### Offer Not Crediting

**Problem**: Completed offer but didn't receive reward

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 24 hours for verification
2. Check your transaction history
3. Ensure you followed all instructions
4. Contact support with:
   - Offer name
   - Completion date/time
   - Screenshots (if available)

---

### Can't Cash Out

**Problem**: Payout request failing

**Common Causes:**
- Balance below $5 minimum
- Unverified email
- Invalid payment details
- Too many recent payouts

**Solutions:**
1. Verify your email address
2. Check minimum balance requirement
3. Verify payment information
4. Wait 24 hours between payouts
5. Contact support if issue persists

---

### Account Suspended

**Problem**: Can't access account

**Common Reasons:**
- Multiple accounts detected
- VPN/proxy usage
- Fraudulent activity
- Terms of service violation

**Solutions:**
1. Review our Terms of Service
2. Contact support to appeal
3. Provide verification if requested
4. Wait for review (1-3 business days)

---

### Verification Email Not Received

**Problem**: Didn't get verification email

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5 minutes and try again
3. Verify email address is correct
4. Request new verification email
5. Contact support if still not received
